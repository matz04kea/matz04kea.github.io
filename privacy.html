Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Karabi M. George Writing Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses and protects personal information submitted through karabigeorge.com (the "Website").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address and message when you contact us through the Website.
  • Newsletter subscriptions — your email address if you choose to subscribe to updates via Substack.

We do not collect any payment information or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • To send newsletter updates and writing dispatches if you have subscribed.
  • We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

This Website uses the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Web3Forms — processes contact form submissions and delivers them to our email inbox. See their privacy policy at web3forms.com/privacy.
  • Substack — manages newsletter subscriptions. See their privacy policy at substack.com/privacy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this Website. See GitHub's privacy policy at github.com.
  • Google Analytics — we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this website. It collects anonymised data including pages visited, time spent on pages, general location (country/city level), device type, and how visitors found the site (e.g. via Google search or social media). Google Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google reCAPTCHA — used on the contact form to prevent spam. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Cookies

This Website uses the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics cookies — used to collect anonymised information about how visitors use this website (pages visited, time on site, traffic sources). These are analytics cookies, not advertising cookies.
  • Google reCAPTCHA cookies — set by Google's spam-prevention service on the contact form.

We do not use advertising, targeting or social media cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings or opt out of Google Analytics at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) and applicable international regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent to receive newsletters at any time by unsubscribing via Substack.

6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 30 days via Web3Forms and then automatically deleted. Newsletter subscriber data is held by Substack and governed by their retention policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us via the Contact page.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
