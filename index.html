Not a Good

NOT A GOOD MOTHER is a fresh, twisty, page-turning thriller that explores the pressures of cultural duty and misogyny and the isolation of motherhood on a recent immigrant — a woman whose child is abducted from a park and who will do anything to get her back, only to discover that there’s more to the kidnapping than she ever could have imagined.
